# Privacy Policy - iCloud Markdown Todo

Last updated: 2026-03-04

## Overview

iCloud Markdown Todo ("the App") is a simple task management application. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Data Storage

- All todo data is stored locally on your device in the app's Documents directory.
- Widget data is shared between the app and its widget extension via App Group container, which remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
